🦆 MVDH — Most Valuable Duck Hunter

The Duck Club's own MVP. Not for the runs you made — for the noughts you bagged. Every duck scores, the rarer the sweeter, and consecutive quacks stack ten-pin style. A leaderboard nobody wants to top, and everybody secretly does.

📐 How MVDH is scored

Base: 5 points a duck — the quack itself.
Golden duck (out first ball, where the ball count is recorded): ×2.
Streak accelerator (ten-pin style): consecutive ducks stack — the Nth in a row adds +5×(N−1). A second on the bounce is a Double (+5), a third a Turkey (+10), and up from there.
Pair (both innings of a match ducks): +10; a King pair (two goldens): +20.
Diamond ducks (out without facing) need ball-by-ball to tell from missing data, so they'll join once the electronic-era feed is folded in. Senior cricket only.
