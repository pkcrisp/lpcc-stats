🦆 MVDH — Most Valuable Duck Hunter

The Duck Club's own MVP. Not for the runs you made — for the noughts you bagged. Every duck scores, the rarer the sweeter, consecutive quacks stack ten-pin style, and a rich spread of categories pile on the bonuses. A leaderboard nobody wants to top.

📐 How MVDH is scored

Base: 5 points a duck — the quack itself.
Category bonuses (+1 each): golden (first ball) · bowled · lbw · stumped · run out · opener · both openers gone · lone duck · only-two-quacks · duck-fest (5+ in the innings) · skipper's quack · carried home (ducked but won anyway).
Streak accelerator (ten-pin style): consecutive ducks stack — the Nth in a row adds +5×(N−1). A second is a Double (+5), a third a Turkey (+10), and up.
Pair (both innings ducks): +10; a King pair (two goldens): +20. Senior cricket only.
