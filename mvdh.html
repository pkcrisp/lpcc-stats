🦆 MVDH — Most Valuable Duck Hunter

The Duck Club's own MVP. Not for the runs you made — for the noughts you bagged. Every duck scores, the rarer the sweeter, consecutive quacks stack ten-pin style, and a rich spread of categories pile on the bonuses. A leaderboard nobody wants to top.

📐 How MVDH is scored

Base: just 2 points a duck — a plain nought barely registers.
Category bonuses (+3 each — this is where it hurts), drawn from the Duck Club boards: golden (first ball) · bowled · lbw · stumped · run out · opener · both openers gone · lone duck · only-two-quacks · duck-fest (5+ in the innings) · skipper's quack · road duck (away) · debutant (first-ever innings) · retirement (bowed out on a nought) · birthday duck · carried home (ducked but won anyway). Stack the categories and a single duck can be agony.
Streak accelerator (ten-pin style): consecutive ducks stack — the Nth in a row adds +5×(N−1). A second is a Double, a third a Turkey, and up.
Pair (both innings ducks): +15; a King pair (two goldens): +30. Senior cricket only.
