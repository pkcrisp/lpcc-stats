📐 How MVP is scored

Batting: 1 pt / 20 runs · +1 fifty · +1 more for a hundred · +1 for 20%+ of the team total · +1 for 8+ boundaries.
Bowling: 1 pt / wicket · +1 three-fer · +1 more five-fer · +1 for 2+ maidens · +1 for economy ≤ 3.00.
Fielding: 1 pt / dismissal (catch, run-out, stumping) · +1 for 3+ in a match.
Bonus: +1 on the winning side · +1 more if you captained the win. Senior matches only. Older seasons predate boundary/maiden/economy tracking, so those bonuses only kick in from the modern era — runs, wickets, fielding and results score throughout.
