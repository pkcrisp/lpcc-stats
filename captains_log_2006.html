🗓️ 23 Sep – 29 Sep 2006 · 2006 — 2 matches

Sun 24 Sep 2006 🧢 Sunday 1st XIv TC Tenterden CC · AwayformDWDLL

Sunday 24 September 2006, and the road took us to Tenterden CC with the sun on our backs and, as it turned out, precious little else in our favour when the coin came down wrong. Lost the toss, put in to field — never the news you want to hear on a Sunday, but there we were.

Tenterden built a solid 192 all out in 47.5 overs, Paul Hope top-scoring with 49, well backed by Richard Pearman's 48 and Fred Pearman's 34. Credit to our bowlers for keeping the innings honest: Tom Murphy was pick of the attack with 3-21, Michael Beeney chipped in 3-26 with three maidens of positively miserly intent, and Thomas Beeney took 1-30. Mark Kokkinos and Paul Brabon both departed for ducks at our hands, which I'm reliably informed pleases a bowling side no end.

Chasing 193, we made surprisingly light work of it — 195/5 from 38.2 overs. Mark Christmas played a fine hand of 80, and Richard Piper matched him stroke for stroke with a well-made 67. I'll confess to rather enjoying leading from the front myself with 80, though I'd never say so in front of the lads more than once.

Richard Piper now sits proudly atop the season's run charts with 661, and Michael Beeney leads the wickets with 16 — both fully deserved. Two points, thoroughly earned, a five-wicket win, and a very pleasant drive home. Verdict: a proper team performance, bat and ball both pulling their weight — more of this, please.

Sat 23 Sep 2006 🧢 1st XIv SC Sidcup CC · HomeformLDLLW
