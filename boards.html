LPCC Ducks Club

Established in 2025, the LPCC Ducks Club celebrates a uniquely cricketing rite of passage—earning a “duck” on the scorecard. More than just a tongue-in-cheek badge of honor, the Ducks Club brings together players (and their families) who’ve joined that exclusive zero club. From Father-and-Son “Duck and Duckling” pairs to siblings sharing a “Two of a Quack” distinction, we commemorate each outing with good humor and camaraderie. Whether you’re a founding member or a newcomer, the LPCC Ducks Club is where every duck counts—and every story of triumph through adversity is shared with pride.
