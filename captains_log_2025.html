It was Sherwood CC, Kent, who visited us on Sunday, and a pleasant, cloudy afternoon it turned out to be — the sort of gentle English weather that seems designed for cricket and little else. I lost the toss, which these days I take as read, and duly found myself leading the lads into the field.

A fine win, all told, and a fine afternoon's cricket. Onwards we go.
