☝️ The Decisions

The appeals upheld — caught, lbw, stumped, run out. Where we have ball-by-ball (2017 on) each is given to the exact umpire: the wicket's over says who was at the bowler's end (caught/lbw), a stumping — a square-leg call — goes to the other umpire, and a run-out is resolved from the runs completed and which batsman was out to work out the end they were caught short at. Before that we only know a match's umpires, not who gave what, so both are credited.
